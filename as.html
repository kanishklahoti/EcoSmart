Accessibility Statement - EcoSmart

EcoSmart is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

1. Conformance Status

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) defines requirements for designers and developers to improve accessibility for people with disabilities. Our website strives to conform to WCAG 2.1, Level AA.

2. Measures to Support Accessibility

  • Accessibility is considered in all new content and features.
  • Regular testing is conducted using automated tools and manual checks.
  • Staff are trained on digital accessibility best practices.

4. Technical Specifications

The accessibility of EcoSmart relies on the following technologies to work with the particular combination of web browser and any assistive technologies or plugins installed on your computer:

  • HTML5
  • CSS3
  • JavaScript
  • ARIA (Accessible Rich Internet Applications) landmarks and roles

5. Limitations and Alternatives

Despite our best efforts, some content may not yet be fully accessible. Examples include:

  • Third-party embedded content that may not meet accessibility standards.
  • Complex data visualizations or charts.
  • Uploaded documents (PDFs, DOCX) that are not fully accessible.

We are actively working to improve accessibility across all content. If you encounter barriers, please use our feedback channels above.

6. Compatibility with Browsers and Assistive Technology

EcoSmart is designed to be compatible with the latest versions of major browsers such as Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. It also supports common assistive technologies including screen readers, magnifiers, and keyboard navigation.

7. Ongoing Efforts

EcoSmart is committed to:

  • Regularly auditing our website for accessibility compliance.
  • Providing training for our developers and content creators.
  • Incorporating accessibility into all future website updates and features.

8. Legal Compliance

This accessibility statement is part of our commitment to comply with national and international accessibility laws, including:

  • The Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, India (2016)
  • Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG 2.1)
  • Other applicable accessibility regulations
